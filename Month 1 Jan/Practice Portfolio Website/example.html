About

Parth Sachin Kulkarni

Shikhar Shingnapur is a renowned, ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva (Shambhu Mahadev) located in the Satara district of Maharashtra, India. Situated on a hill top—from which "Shikhar" (peak) is derived—it is a highly revered pilgrimage site and considered the family deity (Kuldaivat) for many people in Maharashtra, including the Bhosale family.
